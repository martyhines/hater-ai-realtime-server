# QUICK SETUP GUIDE: App Store Promotional Text

## WHERE TO FIND IT:
App Store Connect → Your App → App Store → [Current Version] → Promotional Text

## CURRENT RECOMMENDED TEXT:
"🎉 NEW: Get roasted by 15+ AI personalities! Free tier + chat packs from $3.99. Endless laughs await! 🤖🔥"

## WHY THIS WORKS:
✅ Highlights unique 15+ personalities (competitive advantage)
✅ Shows pricing transparency ($3.99 chat packs)
✅ Creates FOMO with "endless laughs"
✅ Includes engaging emojis
✅ Under 170 character limit (89 characters used)

## A/B TESTING IDEAS:

### Version A (Current):
"🎉 NEW: Get roasted by 15+ AI personalities! Free tier + chat packs from $3.99. Endless laughs await! 🤖🔥"

### Version B (Social Proof):
"⭐ Join 10,000+ users! Get hilarious AI roasts from witty comedians & savage critics. Free daily chats! 🎭"

### Version C (Urgency):
"🚨 Limited time: Free personality unlock! Download now & discover unlimited AI roasting fun! 💬"

### Version D (Technical):
"⚡ Real-time AI chat! Choose from OpenAI, Cohere & Hugging Face. Customizable intensity levels! 🤖"

## ROTATION SCHEDULE:
- Week 1-2: Launch excitement (Version A)
- Week 3-4: Social proof (Version B)
- Week 5-6: Limited offer (Version C)
- Week 7-8: Technical features (Version D)
- Then rotate back...

## TRACKING SUCCESS:
Monitor in App Store Connect:
- Impressions (how many saw it)
- Conversion rate (downloads from views)
- Compare against baseline (no promo text)

## PRO TIPS:
🎯 Update weekly to keep fresh
📱 Test on actual devices (looks different on iPhone vs iPad)
🎨 Use 2-3 emojis max for visual appeal
📊 Track which versions perform best
⏰ Time with app updates or holidays
